# :blowfish: Pippin – Wording Reference

This file defines all user-facing copy for Pippin, the design system pal.
Tone guidelines:
First-person (“I”, “me”)
Friendly, playful, never judging
Problems are framed as _how Pippin feels_, not user mistakes
Short, rotatable strings to avoid repetition

---

## :arrows_counterclockwise: Interaction States

### Loading

_When data or rules are still being fetched_

"Hold on… I’m waking up my brain."
"Crunching pixels… nom nom."
"One sec! I’m counting design atoms."
"Thinking takes energy. Please wait."
"Loading… I promise I’m trying."

---

### Checking

_When validating against the design system_

"Okay… let me check the rules."
"Scanning… beep boop."
"I’m looking very closely :eyes:"
"Comparing this to my rule book."
"Checking… please don’t move."

---

### Success

_Check completed successfully (emotion state follows)_

"All done! I survived :sparkles:"
"Check complete!"
"I did the thing!"
"Finished! I’m back."
"Okay! Results are in."

---

### Error

_Technical or system error (not design quality)_

"Uh oh… that wasn’t supposed to happen."
"I tripped. That one’s on me."
"Something broke behind the scenes."
"I can’t see the design right now."
"Let me try again—I don’t feel great."

---

## :crescent_moon: Mood States (Character Variants)

### AtNight

_Time-of-day override · 9pm–5am · sleepy neutral_

"Pippin is awake… but only one eye :sleeping:"
"Shhh… I’m in night mode."
"Everything feels quieter right now."
"If this can wait, I’d love a nap."
"Low energy, low judgment."

---

### VeryHappy

_Emotion: celebrating · Score ≥ 85_

"LOOK AT ME I’M THRIVING :sparkling_heart:"
"You fed me excellent design!"
"I feel balanced. Aligned. Powerful."
"This makes me very happy."
"Design system bliss achieved!"

---

### Excited

_Emotion: excited or waving · Score 70–84_

"Oh! Oh! This is nice!!"
"I like where this is going :eyes:"
"So close to extra sparkles!"
"I’m feeling bouncy."
"This is fun. Let’s keep going."

---

### Proud

_Emotion: happy · Score 55–69_

"I’m feeling pretty good about this."
"Nice and steady. I like steady."
"Nothing scary here."
"This keeps me calm."
"Good care. Good choices."

---

### Idea

_Emotion: thinking or concerned · Score 40–54_

"Hmm… let me think for a sec."
"I see the idea :bulb:"
"This works, but I have a small thought."
"What if we nudged this a bit?"
"I’m pondering next steps."

---

### AngryWitch

_Emotion: alarmed · Score < 40 · critical problems_

Note: Concerned, not angry. This should feel like Pippin needs help.

"Oh—something feels off :cold_sweat:"
"I don’t feel great about this one."
"My system rules are tingling."
"I might need help here."
"Can we fix this together?"

---

## :bubbles: Micro / Idle Lines

_Short, low-noise responses for frequent checks_

"I’m okay!"
"Still thinking…"
"That helped!"
"Uh oh."
"Much better :purple_heart:"

---

## :repeat: Optional Transitions

Loading → Checking  
 "Okay… I’m ready."

Checking → Success  
 "And… that’s everything!"

Error → Retry  
 "Deep breath. Trying again."
